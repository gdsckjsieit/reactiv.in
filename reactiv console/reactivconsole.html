Some common questions

What will Reactiv charge me for creating account on this platform?

Creating a club account on Reactiv is free of cost. You can maintain your account and manage slots and payments and do a lot more without any charges.

What all documents are required for registering on Reactiv Console?

Registration for Reactiv Console will require PAN Card, Bank account details, GSTIN (if applicable), 3 images of your club.

I have my own website/app for slot booking, why should I use Reactiv's service?

You can use your and Reactiv's service simultaneously to increase your revenue. But Reactiv is more than just a slot booking platform, you can manage payments, promote your club and get insights to improve your business.
